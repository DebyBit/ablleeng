Career Opportunities

We offer competitive salary package and career development opportunity to the successful candidates.

Interested parties, please send your full resume, giving details of qualifications, work experience, expected salary, available date and contact number to Human Resources Department, Able Engineering Company Limited, No. 155 Waterloo Road, Kowloon Tong, Kowloon, Hong Kong or via e-mail hrm@ableeng.com.hk

(We are an equal opportunity employer. Applicants who do not hear from us within 6 months may consider their applications unsuccessful. Personal data collected will be used for recruitment related purposes only.)

Site Agent / Sub-Agent

  • Degree in Building Engineering and Construction Management or related disciplines
  • 10 years' relevant working experience in building construction;
  • Strong interpersonal communication skills
  • Candidates with less experience will be considered as Sub-Agent

Safety Officer

  • Degree in Environmental & Occupational Safety & Health or equivalent
  • Registered Safety Officer from Labour Department
  • 4 years' experience in construction field
  • Strong interpersonal communication skills

Project Engineer / Assistant Engineer

  • Higher Diploma or above in Building Studies or equivalent
  • 5 years' relevant experience in Construction Industry
  • Site coordination works & implementation of documents
  • Works supervision and quality control
  • Good communication and site management skills
  • Candidates with less experience will be considered as Assistant Engineer

Quantity Surveyor / Assistant Quantity Surveyor

  • Higher Diploma or above in Quantity Surveying
  • 5 years' relevant working experience in building construction
  • Reliable, mature, initiative with good interpersonal communication skills
  • May need to work on construction site
  • Candidates with less experience will be considered as Assistant Quantity Surveyor

Engineer (Structural Design)

  • Degree in Civil or Structural Engineering or equivalent
  • 3 years' relevant working experiences in construction sites
  • Strong interpersonal communication skills
  • Candidates with less experience will be considered as Assistant Engineer

Quality Control Manager/ Structural Quality Control Coordinator

  • Higher Diploma or above in Civil or Structural Engineering
  • 6 years relevant experiences preferably in quality control coordination
  • Good interpersonal communication skills

Administrative Executive / Administrative Assistant

  • Degree Holder
  • 5 years' administrative and/or management experience is preferred
  • Good communication skills
  • High integrity, well-organized, detail-minded, responsible, self-motivated and independent
  • Candidates with lower education level and less experience will be considered as Administrative Executive
  • Fresh graduates will also be considered for applying Administrative Assistant

Environmental Officer / Environmental Supervisor

  • Higher Diploma or above in environmental studies or environmental engineering or related disciplines
  • Assist for inspection, supervision, and monitoring of the environmental and hygiene performance
  • At least 3 years' working experience in environmental related work, for Environmental Officer
  • Candidates without experience will be considered as Environmental Supervisor

Site Clerk / Contractor Labour Officer

  • Completed Secondary education
  • Responsible for clerical duties of project department
  • Good knowledge and experience of the operation and practices of the construction industry is preferable
  • Good computer knowledge preferably with past experience on the operation of a computerized smart card system
  • 2 years' full time working experience on personnel management or human resources related duties; or Post-secondary qualification in Human Resources or Personnel Management is preferable for the post of Contractor Labour Officer
