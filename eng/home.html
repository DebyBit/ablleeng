Milestone

1976
Able Engineering Company Limited, formerly known as Yeu Can Engineering Co., Ltd was established in 1976.

1999
ABLE built up a good reputation in the industry and was appointed by the Hong Kong Institution of Engineers to provide Scheme "A" Graduate Training to graduates in 1999. 

2003
ABLE was engaged into several challenging projects in Hong Kong. They were Carcass Contract for Tiu Keng Leng Station Development at Tseung Kwan O and several term contracts under Architectural Services Department.

2005
ABLE was awarded the Fitting Out Works to flats at Yau Mei Court, Yau Tong and the Term Contract works under Architectural Services Department, as well as  the Hospital Authority Term Contract for Minor Works.

2007
ABLE engaged into several Alternation and Addition Projects under Architectural Services Department, which included improvement works to Kowloon Park Swimming Pool, Hong Kong Stadium, Hong Kong Coliseum and Queen Elizabeth Stadium.

2009
More projects under Architectural Services Department were awarded; which included the Design and Construction of a General Outpatient Clinic, an Integrated Community Mental Health Support Services Centre, a Long Stay Care Home in Tin Shui Wai, Conversion of the Red Brick Building into Yau Ma Tei Theatre.
.

2010
ABLE was awarded the North Lantau Hospital project under Architectural Services Department, which won the Green Contractor Silver Award in 2012.

2011
ABLE was engaged with maintenance and A&A works under Architectural Services Department,  Redevelopment of Caritas Medical Centre under Hospital Authority; and  Phase 2 Construction of Public Housing Development at Tung Tau Cottage Areas East under Housing Authority.

2012
The Design and Construction of Redevelopment of Tai Lam Centre for Women, and the construction of Tung Chung Swimming Pool were awarded under Architectural Services Department.

2013
ABLE obtained the Hong Kong Institute of Architects the 'Medal of the Year' and the Hong Kong Institute of Architects Merit award of Hong Kong 2013. In the same year, the Design and Construction works of Tin Shui Wai Hospital was awarded. The project later won the Green Contractor Bronze Award 2015.

2014
ABLE was admitted to the Housing Authority's list as a contractor in Group “NW2”(Confirmed).

2015
The Renovation of Architecture Services Department's new office at 1/F Main Block, APB Centre achieved Platinum rating under BEAM Plus Interiors (BI) v1.0 Accreditation and won the ArchSD Annual Award 2015 – Special Architectural Award (Green Interior).

2016

ABLE was awarded three major projects from Housing Authority, which were Construction of Public Housing Development at Tung Chung Area 39, Eastern Harbour Crossing site Phase 7, and Lai Chi Kok Road – Tonkin Street Phase 1&2.


2017
On 20 February 2017, ABLE was listed on the Main Board of Hong Kong Stock Exchange ( Stock Code : 1627 ). 

2018
Design and Construction of Redevelopment of Queen Mary Hospital, Phase 1 - Main Works at Pok Fu Lam commenced in October.

2019
ABLE was engaged in the Main Contract for the Residential Development at Kai Tak Area 1K (Upper RiverBank, Kai Tak) and Public Housing Development at Tuen Mun Area 54 Sites 1 & 1A (Wo Tin Estate, Tuen Mun). 

2020
Development of Cingleot Premium Logistics Centre at the Hong Kong International Airport and Design and Construction of Joint-user Government Office Building in Area 67, Tseung Kwan O commenced in May and September respectively.

2021
Extension and Conversion to St. Paul's Primary Catholic School at Happy Valley completed this year. Public Housing Development at Yip Wong Road, Tuen Mun and Kai Tak Site 2B2 (Kai Yan Court) commenced from June and August correspondingly.

2022
ABLE was awarded a project from Hong Kong Housing Society, the Public Rental Housing Development at Ting On Street, Kwun Tong.
